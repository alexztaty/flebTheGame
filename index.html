FLEB is a drinking game made by three friends when we were flatmates at uni. We’ve been working on it for around four years.

We bought and played many a drinking game while at uni, and we couldn’t find one that we loved - so we made our own. A lot of the games we found were either very low effort or overly sexual. We wanted something a bit different.

FLEB is a combo of our favourite drinking games from over the years, as well as some we’ve made up and some curveball cards to spice up the competition.

We first played the game through Powerpoint slides, and thought it might be better to turn it into a proper physical game after breaking one of our laptops in the process.

We're sharing this game in the hopes it can serve you as dutifully as it has to us.
